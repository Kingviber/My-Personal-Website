Hi, I'm Gift Tsima — a web developer based in Benoni, South Africa, with a genuine curiosity for how the digital world works. My journey started not with a textbook, but with a burning question: "How do VPNs, HTTP, SSH, and IP addresses actually work?"

That spark of curiosity led me down a rabbit hole — from modifying app themes, to understanding the web, to teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I fell in love with the craft of building things that people actually use.

Today I specialise in frontend development with a growing command of React and modern JavaScript tooling, with Node.js on the backend always in my toolkit. I care deeply about performance, clean code, and experiences that feel intuitive.

My vision: help startups build scalable web applications — without the bloat, without the jargon, and always with the user in mind.
